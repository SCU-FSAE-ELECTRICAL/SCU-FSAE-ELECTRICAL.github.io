# Welcome to SCU FSAE Electrical Team

We are the electrical team of Santa Clara University's Formula SAE team, Bronco Racing. Our mission is to design, build, and race high-performance electric vehicles that push the boundaries of engineering excellence.

## What We Do

The SCU FSAE Electrical Team focuses on:

- **Electric Powertrain Design**: Developing efficient and powerful electric motor systems
- **Battery Management**: Creating safe and reliable battery systems
- **Control Systems**: Implementing sophisticated vehicle control algorithms
- **Data Acquisition**: Collecting and analyzing vehicle performance data
- **Safety Systems**: Ensuring driver and team safety through robust electrical design

## Join Us

We're always looking for passionate students interested in automotive engineering, electrical systems, and racing. Whether you're experienced or just starting out, there's a place for you on our team.
